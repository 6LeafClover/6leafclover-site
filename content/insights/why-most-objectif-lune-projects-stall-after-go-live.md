## The real issue

Most Objectif Lune projects do not fail during implementation.

They drift after go-live because ownership becomes unclear, optimisation stops and workflow design is never revisited.

## What usually goes wrong

- nobody owns the platform after launch
- workflows are built for edge cases rather than scale
- change requests pile up without clear control

## What good looks like

Treat the platform like an operational product, not a one-off project.

That means clear ownership, regular review and a plan for improvement.
